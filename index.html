Dodge

Info:

1. Start with 3 lives

2. Use W, A, S, D or your arrow keys to move up, down, left, or right

3. You must survive for 1 min and 30 sec

4. If you get hit by an enemy you will lose 1 life

5. Once you lose all 3 lives you will be able to restart over

6. If you survive for the whole time you will get the option to get keep going until you lose all lives

Enemy Info

Red: This enemy will follow you

Grey: This enemy will only moves horizontally and vertically randomly

Orange: This enemy is random but it only moves diagonally

Pink: This enemy will follow you but always stop right before it hits you
